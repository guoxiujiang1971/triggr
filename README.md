# Triggr

Automated safety alerts that trigger when you arrive on-site.

## Tech Support

For issues, feature requests, or questions, please open an issue at:

https://github.com/guoxiujiang1971/triggr/issues

## Overview

Triggr detects your arrival at a designated location via geofence, Bluetooth beacon, NFC tag, or WiFi, and automatically plays safety reminders, voice recordings, or alert sounds — no manual action needed.

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0+
- iPhone with NFC capability (for NFC trigger)
- GPS/Location services (for geofence)
- Bluetooth 4.0+ (for beacon)

## Privacy

All data is stored locally on device. No accounts, no servers, no telemetry. Your data never leaves your device.
